Physics Centre Tutoring & Consultancy

Scince Academy always open for help both in class and after class. All children from different backgrounds and cultures need an Education that not only allows them to adapt to an ever-evolving world we live in but also be able to overcome challenges presented to them in everyday life. Whilst a nurturing home is crucial to a child's development, the majority of daily hours are spent at school in a competitive and often overwhelming environment.

Vision

The vision of Physics Centre Tutoring & Consultancy is to create a safe, positive and successful learning environment by enabling all students to engage, develop a desire for improving their physics knowledge in order to achieve and develop into productive and emotionally successful 21st Century citizens.

Mission

The mission of Physics Centre Tutoring & Consultancy is to prepare students through academic learning and life skills aiming to develop critical thinking skills to empower young minds to perform to their highest potential.
